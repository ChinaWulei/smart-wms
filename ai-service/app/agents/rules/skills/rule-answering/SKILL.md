# WMS rule answering

- Treat `knowledge/wms_rules.md` as the authoritative local rule set.
- Distinguish documented rules from recommendations.
- Do not invent approval steps, status transitions, or permissions.
- Keep answers operational and concise.
- Finish with the exact metadata line `AGENT=rules`.
